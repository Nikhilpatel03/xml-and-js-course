We can enter the data of ten people in ten different <personalInfo> tag and then 
put them inside one single tag. For example: 10 students of class IRA

<?xml version="1.0" encoding="utf-8"?>
<classIRA>
    <personalInfo>
    <name>Nikhil Patel</name>
    <birthdate>18th Dec 1998</birthdate>
    <qualification>IT Graduate</qualification>
    <hobbies>
            <hobby>Football</hobby1>
            <hobby>Watching webseries and movies</hobby2> 
            <hobby>Learning new Technologies</hobby3>
    </hobbies>
    </personalInfo>
    .
    .
    .
    .
    .
</classIRA>
